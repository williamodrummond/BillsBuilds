Buy a Building

Here at BillsBuilds we strive to make your
Minecraft World / Server a better place by
empowering your world with one of our
fantastical builds.

We will build everything from small projects
to server spawns. The building team consists
of fairly experienced builders and if in doubt,
feel free to check out our portfolio page!

We also offer a free consultation session
where you can tell us what you desire and
we can inform you what to expect and the
standards you will receive by choosing to work
with us.

Please Be Aware that we may decline large
projects depending on company demand
and the time frame you have provided to us.
